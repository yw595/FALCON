**** Expanded_IPI_Listing.csv ****

Expanded list of protein accessions and their intensities for all
matching proteins (not just the best match). 

http://openwetware.org/wiki/Image:Expanded_IPI_Listing.csv

Based on Supplemental Table S3 - Protein group table, from: Gholami,
A. M., Hahne, H., Wu, Z., Auer, F. J., Meng, C., Wilhelm, M., &
Kuster, B. (2013, August 15). Global Proteome Analysis of the NCI-60
Cell Line Panel. Cell Reports. Retrieved from
http://linkinghub.elsevier.com/retrieve/pii/S221112471300380X

**** (end of summary) ****


**** Entrez_and_IPI_unique.csv ****

Entrez Gene to IPI ID mapping; created from the list of IPI IDs in the
NCI60 proteomic dataset from Gholami et al, as well as genes from
Human Recon 2.02. The conversion was handled by DAVID
(http://david.abcc.ncifcrf.gov/) on 08/22/2013.

http://openwetware.org/wiki/Image:Entrez_and_IPI_unique.csv

**** (end of summary) ****


**** Gholami_Table_S8_mRNA.csv ****
Just a CSV version of Table S8 found in Gholami et al.,
which contains mRNA expression data for the NCI-60 panel.
An LZMA compressed version can be found here:

http://openwetware.org/wiki/Image:Gholami_Table_S8_mRNA.csv.lzma
